## üöÄ Mission & Vision

**The Problem:** Early developmental signs are often missed due to limited access to specialists, subjective observation methods, and the high cost of continuous therapy.
**The Cognicare Solution:** Democratize access to expert-level diagnostics using AI. We turn standard play sessions into clinical data points, allowing for continuous, non-invasive monitoring that adapts to the child's unique developmental curve.

---

## üíé Core Value Proposition

*   **For Specialists:** Reduces diagnostic time by 50% through automated behavioral coding. Provides objective data to support clinical intuition.
*   **For Parents:** Offers peace of mind with 24/7 access to progress tracking and professional guidance. Removes the stigma of therapy by disguising it as play.
*   **For Children:** A stress-free, engaging environment where therapy feels like fun, fostering consistent participation and faster improvement.

---

## ü§ñ The AI Engine: Technology Deep Dive

At the heart of Cognicare lies a sophisticated **Multimodal Analysis Engine** powered by state-of-the-art machine learning models.

*   **Computer Vision (OpenCV):** Tracks micro-expressions, gaze, and motor skills (stiming, coordination) from video feeds.
*   **Multimodal Integration (Phi-4):** Synthesizes visual data with audio cues to provide context-aware analysis.
